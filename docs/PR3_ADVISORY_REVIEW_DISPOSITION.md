# Disposition of PR #3 advisory review

PR #3 is later, post-result advisory material on a sibling branch. It does not
supersede Stage 3 approvals or the Stage 4A authorization and locks. The complete
issue-level evidence and implementation trace is in
`metadata/post_stage4a_review_issue_crosswalk.csv`.

## Retained or corrected

- **R1-R3:** retain governance, checklist-selection, and unobserved-DFO-effort warnings,
  but independent custody is one option; the relevant processes are separate; and true
  surveyed negatives are model-specific rather than a universal Stage 4A prerequisite.
- **R5-R7:** directional hypotheses are permissible with nonselective reporting; point
  geometry is error-prone rather than wholly unidentified; the actual cyclic placebo
  path is audited. A different ±14-day critique is not attributed to Stage 4A.
- **R9-R12:** component comparability, multiplicity, misclassification, and route
  footprint are model-specific. A confirmed pooling-family defect and nonconforming
  sensitivity fits replace blanket claims.
- **R13-R17:** the generic helper defects are future-path engineering issues. Their
  hardened implementations are retained, but they were not on the Stage 4A path.
- **R19-R22 and R25-R31:** retain distinct estimands, interference, selection,
  positivity, timing, claim-hierarchy, and leakage concerns with model-specific gates.

## Retired or not applicable

- **R8 anti-chaining:** retired as a category error for the Stage 3 validation-block
  union. A validation block is not a biological-event assertion.
- **R18:** single primary guild membership is not an engineering defect; multi-label
  traits can be separate mechanism metadata.
- **R23:** target-group background is retired as a category error for DFO
  exposure-observation correction.
- **R24:** “fusion” is retired as unsupported transfer; Stage 4A did not use a generic
  herring distance kernel.
- Generic fixes to `zero_fill_taxa()`, linkage, event identity, and kernels have
  `NO_STAGE4A_IMPACT` because the protected builder used a separate path.

## Prospective only or unresolved

Negative-control composition and thresholds, exact placebo rules, a small primary
claim hierarchy, stronger holdout access controls, outcome-free power, optional
time-varying covariates, and any new geometry or observation-process model are
post-result proposals. They may govern the untouched 2026-2028 confirmation only after
explicit human approval and freezing. DFO surveyed-negative comparisons remain an
external-data dependency.

The PR #3 prose files retain historical value on their source branch. They should not be
merged under their existing `docs/14` and `docs/15` names because those names collide
with PR #2's Stage 4A historical methods/results documents. This annotated disposition,
not a silent rewrite of PR #3, is the reconciliation-branch authority statement.
