# Repository agent rules

- Treat raw EBD/SED and record-level derivatives as restricted.
- Never commit source checklist, observer, locality, or exact coordinate identifiers.
- Preserve raw inputs and checksums.
- Declare every join cardinality and test it.
- Keep detection, numeric count, `X`, lower-bound count, and ambiguity distinct.
- Missing herring components are not zero; relative spawn index is not absolute biomass.
- Use all concurrent event links for additive exposure; do not silently duplicate checklists as independent rows.
- All prespecified models are reported regardless of sign.
- Hard stops and warnings follow `docs/04_DECISION_RULES.md`.
- Current data analyses are exploratory/estimand-refining until prospective confirmation.
- Stage 3 Phases 1-3 are complete and human-approved. Stage 4A was executed under the
  recorded authorization and is pending human scientific review; preserve its v1 specs,
  locks, execution record, outputs, and hashes as immutable history.
- Stage 4B is not automatically authorized. Use the estimand- and model-specific gates
  in `metadata/estimand_progression_gate.csv` and `metadata/model_progression_gate.csv`;
  do not apply a blanket rule that every former S2 concern blocks every response model.
- M31 and all 2026-2028 response records remain locked until the complete prospective
  release and an approved execution package. Never use an interim holdout response look.
- PR #3 is advisory post-result material and cannot supersede an approved or hashed
  record. Any later amendment must disclose that Stage 4A development results were known.
- Do not rerun protected Stage 4A models or open untracked protected response derivatives
  without new explicit human authorization. Corrections are additive and versioned.
