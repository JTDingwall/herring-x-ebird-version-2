# Repository agent rules

- Treat raw EBD/SED and record-level derivatives as restricted.
- Never commit source checklist, observer, locality, or exact coordinate identifiers.
- Preserve raw inputs and checksums.
- Declare every join cardinality and test it.
- Keep detection, numeric count, `X`, lower-bound count, and ambiguity distinct.
- Missing herring components are not zero; relative spawn index is not absolute biomass.
- Use all concurrent event links for additive exposure; do not silently duplicate checklists as independent rows.
- All prespecified models are reported regardless of sign.
- Hard stops and warnings follow `docs/04_DECISION_RULES.md`.
- Current data analyses are exploratory/estimand-refining until prospective confirmation.

## Scientific inference discipline (reviewer-mandated)

- Do not assume a response direction for any taxon. A broad, inclusive candidate list is
  encouraged; a presumed positive effect is prohibited. `expected_direction` is a
  prespecified hypothesis label for placebo and consistency checks only — never a prior
  belief and never an inclusion criterion.
- "Most species respond" is a testable prediction, not a design assumption. It is evaluated
  against a strengthened negative-control panel, and there must be a prespecified decision
  rule for a non-null falsification result.
- Exposure and eBird sampling share one human-effort process, and observers are drawn to
  visible spawn. Report the visitation/allocation diagnostic beside every biological
  estimand, and cap claims at "recorded-event vs no-recorded-active-event, conditional on
  observed sampling." DFO spawn records are presence-only; "no recorded active event" is not
  confirmed absence.
- Placebo date shifts must exceed the maximum registered egg-availability kernel.
- H2 (distance decay) and H5 (extent) are not identified from source-point geometry alone;
  register a nearest-point-on-spawn-line sensitivity where shoreline coverage permits.
- Confirmatory status requires external pre-registration and an independent data custodian
  for the prospective holdout. Internal hashing establishes tamper-evidence, not
  independence.

## Reviewer-mandated remediation gates

- `docs/14_SCIENTIFIC_REVIEW_AND_REMEDIATION.md` is the authoritative remediation register.
- No response model (Phase 4) is fit until every S2 item in that register is resolved.
- No confirmatory claim is made until every S1 item is resolved.
