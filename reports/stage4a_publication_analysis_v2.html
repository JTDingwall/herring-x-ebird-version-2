Model disposition and exploratory work

M26 v1 is retired from the inferential publication set without replacement because it lacks a registered exposure or visitation contrast. Outcome-informed follow-up analysis is permitted only when labeled exploratory, dated relative to outcome inspection, and accompanied by multiplicity reporting where a hypothesis family is evaluated. All prespecified results remain reported regardless of sign.

Limitations and claim boundary

These are checklist-conditional associations, not population abundance, biomass, occupancy, migration, movement, or causal effects. Checklist submission, observer behavior, residual spatial-temporal confounding, interference among concurrent spawn events, and incomplete DFO monitoring remain limitations. All concurrent event links contribute additively. Missing herring components are not zero; missing DFO records are not surveyed negatives, and unmonitored coverage remains unknown.
