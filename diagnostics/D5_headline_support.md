# D5 — Per-species support and prevalence

Full table `D5_species_support_prevalence.csv` (detections, positive numeric
counts, and detection prevalence over the 217,200 modeled checklists). All 49
core species and 2 comparators are well supported; only one non-modeled taxon in
the sparse-state file fell under the suppression threshold.

## The three headline species (asked for explicitly)

The manuscript gives the minimum panel joint-cell support (259 rows) but nothing
for the species carrying the largest effects. They are not thinly supported:

| Species | Detections | Positive numeric counts | Detection prevalence |
|---|---|---|---|
| American Herring Gull | 1,957 | 1,851 | 0.90% |
| Iceland Gull | 4,763 | 4,317 | 2.19% |
| Long-tailed Duck | 3,070 | 2,946 | 1.41% |

For comparison, the panel spans Glaucous-winged Gull (89,556 detections) down to
White-winged Scoter (4,935). The headline species sit at the lower end of panel
prevalence but still rest on roughly two to five thousand detections and positive
counts each. Recommended in-text support figures for Phase 3 item 8.
