# Marine Environmental Research manuscript v6

The complete Strait of Georgia event-study family was executed and received a
scientific decision of **pass with qualifications**. Version 6 is a
Strait of Georgia-only, post-result ecological refinement.

Files:

- `mer_manuscript_unblinded_v6.qmd`: 8,000-9,000-word ecology manuscript;
- `mer_supplement_v6.qmd`: complete-family status, specificity, failures, and
  immutable Stage 4A M05/M08 inventory;
- `mer_references_v6.bib`: author-year reference library;
- `mer_v6_times_new_roman_reference.docx`: Word style reference used by both
  manuscript sources to enforce Times New Roman.

The print stylesheet applies Times New Roman to the HTML/PDF render, and the
Word reference document applies the same family to DOCX styles.

The main interpretation uses the duration-weighted 0-14 day
near/reference difference-in-differences and retains checklist detection and
conditional finite positive flock size as distinct outcomes. All 11 main
species are shown. Gadwall and Northern Shoveler remain supplemental specificity
comparators, with the positive Northern Shoveler interaction disclosed as
evidence of residual confounding.

Historical Stage 4A results, specifications, locks, outputs, and hashes remain
unchanged. Current results are exploratory and estimand-refining until
prospective confirmation.
