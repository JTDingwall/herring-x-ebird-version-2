# Suggested reviewer expertise

The manuscript would benefit from reviewers collectively covering:

- coastal and marine bird ecology;
- Pacific herring or forage-fish spawning ecology;
- eBird and semistructured community-science inference;
- hierarchical and mixed-effects ecological models;
- spatial and temporal exposure linkage;
- falsification, negative-control, and sensitivity-analysis design; and
- reproducible, privacy-aware ecological workflows.

No individual reviewers are proposed because the author supplied no names. Editors should avoid reviewers with recent collaboration, supervisory, financial, institutional, or personal conflicts with the author.
