# Reporting checklist and applicability

No study-design-specific reporting checklist is mandated in the current *Marine Environmental Research* Guide for Authors.

| Item | Status | Location or rationale |
|---|---|---|
| Registered observational questions and estimand | Complete | Introduction; Methods |
| Eligibility and observation-process covariates | Complete | Methods |
| Response-state definitions | Complete | Methods; Supplement |
| Exposure and concurrent-event linkage | Complete | Methods; Supplement |
| Multiplicity and family definitions | Complete | Methods; machine-readable audit |
| All prespecified models reported | Complete | Results; Supplement; companion CSVs |
| Singular-fit disclosure | Complete | Methods; Results; Supplementary Table S6 |
| M29 specificity panel | Complete | Abstract; main Results and Discussion |
| Limitations paired with affected interpretation | Complete | Limitations |
| Data/code availability | Complete | End matter; author declarations |
| Funding, conflict, contributions, ethics | Complete subject to human confirmation token | End matter; author declarations |
| SAGER sex/gender reporting | Not applicable | Checklist-level bird outcomes; no sex/gender analysis of humans or animals |
| Graphical abstract | Optional, not supplied | Encouraged rather than required |
